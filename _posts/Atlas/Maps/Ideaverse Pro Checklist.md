# Key Lessons in the Online Course


### How to Add New Information (+)
- [ ] Complete [How to add ideas you get](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533422/lessons/2060338) 

### How to Manage Ideas & Knowledge (Sensemaking)
- [ ] Complete [How to add and view things like quotes in powerful, semi-automated ways (Collections & Views)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533416/lessons/2003924) 
- [ ] Complete [How to save and organize the words and works of other people (Sources)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533416/lessons/2060372) 

### How to Manage Your Past, Present, & Future (Time Traveling)
- [ ] Complete [How to plan your day and reflect on it (Daily Note)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533420/lessons/2060311) 
- [ ] Complete [How to record meeting notes and other things that matter (Records)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533420/lessons/2060405) 
- [ ] Complete [How to view all your calendar notes in chronological order (Calendar Views)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533420/lessons/2003925) 
- [ ] Complete [How to review your progress after time has passed (Reviews)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533420/lessons/2003931)  

### How to Manage Efforts, Projects, & Shared Works (Actiontaking)
- [ ] Complete [Understanding the efforts in your life (Efforts)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533418/lessons/2060438) 
- [ ] Complete [How to manage all the areas of efforts in your life (Areas)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533418/lessons/2003932) 
- [ ] Complete [How to manage projects with three levels of intensity (Projects)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533418/lessons/2003934) 
- [ ] Complete [How to save and reference things you've created and shared (Works)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533418/lessons/2003936) 

### How to Support Your Ideaverse (Extras)
- [ ] Complete [How to add packs of knowledge to your ideaverse (Packs)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533421/lessons/2060446) 
- [ ] Complete [How to standardize the notes you make (Templates)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533421/lessons/2060447) 
- [ ] Complete [Where to put your images and drawings (Visuals)](https://community.linkingyourthinking.com/c/ideaverse-pro/sections/533421/lessons/2060449)
